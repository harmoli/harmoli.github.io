about

Harmony Mingwai Li is currently a Masters candidate in Computer Graphics and Game Technology (CGGT) at the University of Pennsylvania. Her main academic interests are GPU algorithms, distributed systems, and physically based simulations. In July, she will join Pixar's studio tools group as a software engineer.

She recently graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a BSE in Computer Science. During her time as an undergraduate, she contributed the CIS@Penn and pursued her passion for teaching as head teaching assistant for an introductory level computer science course. As a graduate student, she continued to teach as a teaching assistant for graduate level computer graphics courses.

As an undergraduate, she has interned at Amazon (AWS, AppStream), Microsoft (Exchange Server, Outlook Web App), and Ipreo (Variable Rate Trading).
